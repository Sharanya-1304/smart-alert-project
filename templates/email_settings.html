Quick Setup (2 minutes):

Troubleshooting
Q: Where do I get the App Password?

A: Gmail requires a special 16-character App Password (not your regular Gmail password). Get it at: https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords

Q: "2-Step Verification" is disabled?

A: You must enable 2FA first before generating an App Password. Go to: https://myaccount.google.com/security

Q: Emails still not working?

A: After saving the password, click "Send Test Email" to verify it works. Check the caregiver email inbox (including Spam folder).

Q: What if I don't have a Gmail account?

A: You can still use the system. Notifications will be logged to email_queue.txt. A future update will support other email providers.
